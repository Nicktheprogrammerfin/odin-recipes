What Is A Peanut Butter And Jelly??

A Peanut Butter And Jelly is a sandwich consisting of a fruit jelly, jam, or preserve, mixed with peanut butter (creamy or crunchy, although crunchy is the best). It is the essential childhood sandwich, and is still a 10/10, top tier sandwich to this day.

Well, How Do I Make One???

It's easy! All You'll need is:

  • Peanut Butter (crunchy or creamy)
  • A Jelly, Jam or Preserve (my go to is strawberry preserves)
  • Bread!

Okay, Got Those. Now What????

Really? Okay, let me walk you through this...

  1. Lay two pieces of bread out on a plate.
  2. Spread crunchy peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread strawberry preserves on the other slice of bread.
  4. Stick the two halves together.
  5. (Optional) Slice In Half, either across the bread in the middle or diagonally.

...and bam! You've got yourself a good ol' fashioned PB&J.
