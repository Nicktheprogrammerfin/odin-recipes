Eggs Benedict

What is Eggs Benedict?

a dish consisting of poached eggs and sliced ham on toasted English muffins, covered with hollandaise sauce (that's the key).

This Recipe Consists Of:

  • 2 English Muffins
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 4 Slice of Canadian Bacon
  • Vinegar (just a splash)

(For The Hollandaise Sauce):

  • 4 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 2 Teaspoons of Lemon Juice (or Lime)
  • 1 Tablespooon of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Salt And Pepper To Taste

Steps:

First, The Hollandaise Sauce
  1. Start making the Hollandaise sauce by melting butter in a saucepan.
  2. Meanwhile, beat egg yolks in separate bowl and add lime juice, heavy cream, and salt and pepper.
  3. Once the butter has melted, youâ€™re ready to temper the eggs by adding a small amount of the hot butter to the egg mixture.
  4. Stir it well and repeat this process, slowly adding one spoonful of hot butter to the egg mixture (We do this to avoid curdling the eggs).
  5. Finally, add the mixture back to the saucepan and cook it for a few more seconds.
Now The Poached Egg
  1. It's time to poach an egg. First, fill the pot up with about 3 inches of water.
  2. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce heat until it reaches a simmer.
  3. Add a little splash of vinegar to the water (to help the egg white stay together).
  4. Crack one egg into a small cup, or measuring cup.
  5. Lower the egg into the simmer water, gently easing it out of the cup.
  6. Cook the egg in simmering water for 3-5 minutes, depending on how soft you like youir egg yolk.
  7. Remove the poached egg with a slotted spoon.
Almost There!
  1. Warm The Canadian Bacon.
  2. Toast The English Muffin.
  3. Place the Canadian Bacon on top of half of a toasted English Muffin
  4. Place one poached egg on top of the Canadian bacon, and then smother with Hollandaise Sauce.

And... viola! You now know how to prepare an Eggs Benedict.
